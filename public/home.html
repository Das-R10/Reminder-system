UPI, bank transfer, or international wire. After you select a plan, you'll get exact payment details. Your plan activates within minutes of payment confirmation.

Yes! You can subscribe to WhatsApp + SMS independently, or save with a bundle. Each channel is tracked separately.

Yes. For WhatsApp Business API (via Gupshup), your messages use pre-approved templates. We help you set these up.
